Willow Designs Website Proposal

Strategy

  • Creating this website to showcase my business and sell jewelry
  • I am offering my audice a chance to see my jewelry and purchase a piece
  • I would like people to be able to purchase necklaces on my site
  • I want a simple and clean design (along with great products) to bring people back

General Site Description

  • Point of sale site
  • It will have a gallery of products, a way to purchse products, info about the Willow Designs, and a contact me section
  • My most important message is to get my brand across as a desirable and fun way to accessorize

Target Audience

  • Primary audience is women ages 14-55
  • This is a large group so the younger side is pretty internet-savvy but the older group may not be
  • I would assume the younger part of the audience will have smaller screens and the older part will use larger screens
  • I might expect someone to visit my site maybe once every 2-3 months and spend at least 5 minutes on the site

Content

  • I am responsible for content generation
  • Content will be updately at least weekly, if not more frequently

Resources

  • I will spend the amount of time required to keep the website up to date
  • I have a server for my site and have registered my domain mane

Graphic Look and Feel

  • I would like a simple and clean look to the site
  • There are existing logos and colors that need to be incorpoated into the site
  • This site is not part of a larger site
  • I like the images, design, and layoutof Helbak Ceramics' site
